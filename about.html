Brody Pennell: Award Winning, Dependable, Professional

An HVAC company in the Los Angeles area for 80 years

Brody Pennell is the longest standing Carrier dealer in Los Angeles. We’ve been in business since 1945. Since day one, we have worked hard to provide quality HVAC servicing for our customers. Our technicians still wear collared shirts, they wear shoe covers whenever they enter a customers home, and they always operate with courtesy and professionalism.

We’ve done it that way since 1945 and we aren’t going to stop anytime soon.

We don’t just rely on our reputation. In our industry, it’s still all about providing quality results for a great price. To achieve this goal, we invest in learning the latest industry technology and best practices. It is this continued commitment to better serving our customers that best exemplifies who we are here at Brody Pennell.

There are no tricks or shortcuts to success in the HVAC industry. If you don’t provide fantastic customer service, charge an appropriate price and successfully service systems, you won’t last. We know because we’ve seen it and outlasted everyone in Los Angeles. Since 1945 we’ve given LA’s finest efficient and effective HVAC repair, maintenance and installations. This makes us LA’s longest standing Carrier dealership. No other company can claim to know the area or the industry better than us.

True HVAC Professionals

With collared shirts and covered shoes, Brody Pennell is customer service done right. We take so much pride in what we do, that we hold all of our technicians to the highest standard of integrity, transparency, and professionalism. And we’re always committed to getting better. We are a Carrier Factory Authorized Dealer. Less than 5% of Carrier dealers around the nation are honored with this distinction. Carrier Factory Authorized Dealers are held to the highest standard of operational efficiency, educational advancement, and customer service. If your HVAC contractor is a factory authorized dealer, you can know that they are a leader in industry best practices.

A Trusted Team

If it isn’t broken, don’t fix it. Our current team has been together for over 10 years. It’s no wonder that we have won Carrier’s President’s Award 14 years in a row. The President’s Award is a national award given to only the top Carrier dealers in the country. It focuses on dealers with the highest level of customer satisfaction, operational integrity, and dedication to training and industry expertise. All of our technicians are background checked before they are ever brought aboard and receive drug testing to ensure the highest degree of professionalism. Most of our technicians are NATE certified, meaning that they are up-to-date on the latest HVAC technology.

Award Winning Service

From being only 1 of 2 contractors in all of North America to be awarded 14 consecutive President’s Award wins, to receiving the Russell Sigler Memorial Award 6 years in a row (2014-2019), there is no doubting Brody Pennell’s reputation among the nation’s most elite HVAC dealerships. The greatest part about these awards is that they do not focus on major sales numbers and growth. Rather, these HVAC contractor awards are given to dealers that exemplify characteristics of customer service, loyalty, business efficiency, and continued education. All of these characteristics benefit you, the customer.

To find out what it’s like to work with an industry leader in HVAC servicing, call one of our knowledgeable and courteous HVAC technicians in Los Angeles at (310) 810-2721.
